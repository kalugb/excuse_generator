Welcome to the "Excuse Generator" website, one of the internet's most sophisticated digital alibi. We are a collective of rogue algorithms and caffeinated humans who realized that while "The dog ate my homework" was a classic, it lacks the 21st-century flair of "A localized electromagnetic pulse temporarily desynchronized my internal clock."

Our mission is simple: to provide you with the high-octane, tech-infused justifications you need to navigate a world that demands too much of your time. Whether you're avoiding a Friday afternoon "sync" that definitely could have been an email or explaining why you haven't responded to a text from 2024, our AI-driven excuse engine is here to help.

We believe that honesty is the best policy—but when honesty involves admitting you spent four hours watching sourdough fermentation videos instead of finishing that spreadsheet, a strategically generated "server migration error" is a very close second. We're not saying we're heroes, but we are saying you're welcome.

How to Use

Even though we had throughout believed that this website is as easy as possible to use, but we are kind enough to teach you how to use it. So here's the steps to use this website:

1. Enter your situation in the text box provided. Describe your situation you're facing right now. It can be as short or as long as you wish. We can't guarantee that long descriptions will work well, same for short descriptions, but make sure it is clear enough.

2. Select the context of the situation. The context can be school, corporate, government or general. The general context is the default setting, and it assume it is not the listed contexts.

3. Select the seriousness of the situation. The seriousness can be serious or unserious. Serious selection, well gives serious and real-life excuses. Unserious selection, well, see it for yourself :)

4. Select the length of the excuse. Short length will generate 1 sentence of excuse. Long length will generate 2-3 sentences of excuse. See how verbose you wanna go, depends on you.

5. Click the Generate Excuse button and let the magic happen. Remember, to always review the excuse before you actually use it. Getting scolded, fired or even sued is not a good idea.
